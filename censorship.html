About Combatting Censorship in North Korea

Censorship in North Korea is a significant issue that restricts freedom of expression and information. This website aims to shed light on the importance of fighting against this censorship and promoting a more open society.

By combatting censorship, we can help North Koreans access diverse information, express their opinions, and advocate for their rights. Join us in this vital cause!
